Catronauts
In Catronauts, you and up to 3 of your friends will work together to safeguard a mothership loaded with precious cargo travelling through hostile space!

Armed with only a deadly laser pointer and a yarn lasso, Catronauts pits you against multitudes of absurd obstacles and enemies. Guide catkind through ever-increasing challenges by learning to rely on your teammates, because you’re always better when you work together.

About This Game

Adventuresome from start to finish, Catronauts opens a window into a world of adorable heroes and comical villains. To protect the newly dispossessed cat federation, you’ll face off against cunning rats, diabolical dogs and more. Catronauts challenges players to overcome incredible odds through cooperation, and makes for a great experience for gamers of any age.
